About Me

I am Nurependi. I have 3 years of experience in web development, particularly in building efficient and logical systems with a focus on core functions. For interfaces, I often adapt and integrate existing templates.

Additionally, I have 5 years of experience in 3D modeling, and currently, I focus on creating 3D assets and environments ready for use in game development.

My expertise in web architecture and 3D game design allows me to contribute to both the technical and visual aspects of digital projects. I believe that strong systems are supported by solid technical implementation, complemented by effective visuals. Outside of work, I enjoy 3D modeling and playing games, which contribute to the development of my skills and creativity.
